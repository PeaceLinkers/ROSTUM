About Me

A self taught composer on a journey through music.

I'm Rostum Kawai, a self-taught composer driven by passion and creativity. My musical journey began with the piano, where I taught myself to play and express emotions through melodies. Over time, I explored digital composition using FL Studio, which opened new doors for experimentation and sound design.

Since then, I've composed original tracks for various short films and indie games, blending classical influences with modern electronic elements. Music for me is not just sound—it's a story, a feeling, a world. I continue to grow as an artist with every project, always exploring new sounds and pushing creative boundaries.
